Add AI visibility to what your agency delivers

Only 1 in 4 agencies currently offers AI visibility as a client service. But client interest is growing faster than most teams are equipped to respond. SE Ranking fills that gap

STEP 1
Find the opportunity before the pitch

Review a prospect’s AI visibility, spot gaps in brand mentions and cited sources, and use those findings to frame a sharper sales conversation.

STEP 2
Run an AI visibility audit

Check how the brand appears across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, AI Mode, and AI Overviews. See which prompts trigger mentions, which sources get cited, and where visibility is absent

STEP 3
Shape a stronger optimization strategy

See how competitors appear across the same AI answers, identify prompt and citation patterns driving their visibility, and define where your client has an opportunity to improve presence

STEP 4
Track AI results over time

Monitor mention frequency and citation patterns across engines over time, so you can show clients that AI visibility work is moving.

STEP 5
Report it as a service

Package findings, progress, and next steps into client-ready reporting that makes AI visibility a recurring conversation.
