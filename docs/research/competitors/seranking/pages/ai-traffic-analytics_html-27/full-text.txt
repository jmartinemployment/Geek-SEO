FAQ
Q
What is the AI Traffic Analytics?
A

AI Traffic Analytics by SE Ranking lets you track and analyze website traffic from AI chatbots like ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Claude, and more. It shows how users from these platforms interact with your site, and covers sessions, engagement, bounce rates, and other key metrics. The data is pulled directly from GA4 through SE Ranking’s integration and displayed in clear, visual dashboards. You can also assess your brand’s visibility across AI-powered platforms and analyze competitor performance.

Q
Why should I track AI chatbot-driven traffic?
A

Traffic from AI chatbots is growing. For example, in 2025, ChatGPT generated nearly 4x more traffic than in 2024, which can impact your strategy. By tracking this traffic, you’ll understand how AI-referred visitors behave, which pages they land on, and how well they engage. This will help you optimize content, prioritize optimization efforts, and get more visitors from AI chatbots.

Q
Which AI chatbots can I analyze with the AI Traffic Analytics?
A

You can analyze traffic from AI chatbots like ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Claude, and more. In addition, you can track and research website and brand visibility in these systems, plus Google’s AI Mode and AI Overviews.

Q
Can I compare AI chatbot traffic to other traffic sources?
A

Yes, you can analyze your AI chatbots traffic share and compare it with other sources side by side. This allows you to evaluate performance in context and prioritize your efforts.
