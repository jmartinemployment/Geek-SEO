FAQ
Q
What’s the best tool to understand my visibility in ChatGPT answers?
A

SE Ranking’s ChatGPT Visibility Tracker is one of the first tools built specifically for this purpose, and so far, it’s one of the best for tracking brand visibility in ChatGPT. It helps you see how often your brand is mentioned in ChatGPT answers, how it is described, whether links are included, and how your competitors perform. The tool is designed to give you accurate data and automate the tracking process.

Q
How reliable or accurate is the visibility data?
A

SE Ranking’s ChatGPT visibility tracking software delivers high-accuracy data by analyzing outputs from ChatGPT for your target keywords. We frequently update information, capture actual responses, and collect mentions and links. Moreover, you can see how metrics change over time with historical data, so you can trust the numbers you’re getting and make informed decisions.

Q
How does this tool detect and measure brand mentions in ChatGPT responses?
A

The tool scans ChatGPT answers for your target keywords and analyzes which of them end up with your brand being mentioned in the response. You need to specify your brand name in the setup window when adding queries for tracking. It can be a business, a branded product, a person, or a place. Then you can monitor ChatGPT mentions to see how often your brand is featured over time and whether the answer links to your website.

Q
Does it track mentions across different LLMs (e.g., Claude, Gemini), or just ChatGPT?
A

This tool is designed for ChatGPT mention monitoring. SE Ranking also has a similar solution for AI Overviews tracking, and AI Mode Tracker. These tools provide accurate brand tracking, deep insights, competitor monitoring, historical data, and more. SE Ranking plans to support other AI systems like Perplexity, Gemini, Claude, and more with the same level of precision. 

Q
Can I track competitors or related keywords in addition to my own brand?
A

Yes, you can monitor the names of companies, products, people, and places. Just specify what you need in the tool setup menu. You will also see which competitor brands are featured in the same ChatGPT responses, along with yours, and compare results side-by-side. 

Q
Does the tool show examples of actual mentions within ChatGPT answers?
A

Yes, it does. You can review real responses from ChatGPT to see how exactly your brand or competitors are mentioned. This gives you full context, letting you know how your brand is described.
