FAQ
Q
How does the AI Search Toolkit work?
A

The AI Search Toolkit monitors AI-generated answers tied to the prompts you track. It also checks for linked and unlinked references to brands, products, businesses, or personal names. You’ll see if your website or brand is mentioned, whether it’s linked or not, and where it appears among the sources. You can also monitor how often your competitor domains or any sites of interest are cited, so you can spot trends in visibility over time.

 

Q
What are the key features of the tool?
A

SE Ranking’s AI Search Toolkit covers the full AI visibility workflow:

Brand mention and link tracking across AI Overviews, AI Mode, ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity.
Competitor AI visibility monitoring with side-by-side analysis and deeper research in LLMs.
Source data to see which domains and URLs are featured in AI-generated answers.
Cached AI answer copies to see how LLMs frame brands.
Historical data to monitor how AI visibility evolves over time and catch shifts.
Q
What’s the difference between the AI Search Toolkit and traditional SEO tools?
A

Traditional SEO tools check to see if your website links appear for targeted keywords in the search engine results page. The AI search grader focuses on generative engine optimization (GEO). It monitors answers generated by ChatGPT, Google’s AIOs, and others. Here are the metrics it tracks: 

Mentions in AI answer texts with and without site links. It captures brand mentions and other references. 
Links to websites that AI use the most to generate answers and that appear most often in the cited sources section.
Citation dynamics, traffic driven by AIOs, and average ranking within generated responses.

AI Search Toolkit is a leading software built for SEO pros. It blends classic workflows with the latest in Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) and combines AI brand tracking, competitive analysis for AI search visibility, and LLM response evaluation. And you get all of this on one intuitive platform. Get the data you need to stay visible and competitive in AI search.

Q
What is a “good” AI visibility score?
A

There’s no benchmark yet as AI search is too new for the industry. What matters more than hitting a specific number is understanding your score in context. You can compare your visibility against direct competitors in your niche and check if you are appearing in AI answers for your core prompts. If competitors are outranking you in mentions and links, that’s the gap to focus on. You can create content that AI is likely to cite or partner with other resources that LLMs already mention.

Q
Can I track the visibility of specific prompts?
A

Yes, you can use SE Ranking’s AI Results Tracker to monitor AI brand mentions and links for your target prompts. It shows whether your brand appears in AI answers triggered by your target queries, where it ranks, and how your visibility changes over time.

Q
What’s the AI Competitor Research, and how does it help?
A

The AI Competitor Research Tool analyzes any domain’s visibility in AI answers. It shows how often that domain is cited or mentioned, tracks citation frequency, collects average positions and prompts.

Q
Why do I need to optimize my website for AI search engines?
A

Traditional search engines still matter, but AI engines like ChatGPT, Gemini, and Claude are attractive to users because they give quick, direct answers. Since more people are using AI, not being mentioned in them could hurt your overall visibility. Tracking AI results and optimizing for them keeps your brand visible to clients who use AI search tools.

Q
How do I measure the ROI of AI search visibility?
A

ROI from AI visibility is still hard to measure, but you can start looking for correlations between your LLM visibility and changes in other channels. Monitor whether increases in AI mentions and links correlate with growth in branded search traffic. Use historical data to identify which prompts or topics are driving the most visibility, then map those back to your content investments to see what’s working.

Q
Does the tool use API or UI to get the results?
A

SE Ranking’s AI Search Toolkit retrieves results through direct UI-based monitoring. It sends queries to AI platforms and captures the responses as a real user would see them. This means the data reflects live AI behavior rather than sampled outputs.

Q
Which AI search systems does the tool analyze?
A

SE Ranking’s AI Search Toolkit analyzes answers generated by ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, AI Mode, and AI Overviews.
