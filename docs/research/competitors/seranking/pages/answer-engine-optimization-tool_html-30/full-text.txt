FAQ
Q
What is answer engine optimization (AEO)?
A

Answer engine optimization (AEO) is a strategy for improving your visibility in answer engines like ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, etc. The goal is to make sure your brand, website, and content get mentioned and linked when users conversate with and ask questions to AI tools.

Q
How does the Answer Engine Optimization Tool work?
A

Our AEO Tool tracks how answer engines mention and link your brand when responding to user prompts. It shows which of them result in your content, brand, or website being featured, how often you’re mentioned, how you stand against competitors, and how your visibility changes over time. It’s one of the leading answer engine optimization platforms for AI visibility because it helps you do similar research on your competitors to learn how to improve your presence.

Q
Which AI platforms does your AEO Tool analyze?
A

SE Ranking’s AEO Tool currently analyzes visibility across Google AI Overviews and ChatGPT. We gradually expand the list of LLMs to ensure we go deeper and deliver better-quality data. AI Mode, Gemini, Perplexity, and Claude support is coming soon with the same level of precision.

Q
Can I access AEO data programmatically or integrate it into my own tools?
A

Yes. SE Ranking offers both an API and an MCP Server for programmatic access to AEO data. The API lets you pull brand mentions, AI visibility scores, and competitor data directly into your dashboards, reports, or workflows. The MCP Server takes this further — it lets AI assistants like Claude, tools like Cursor, and automation platforms like n8n query SE Ranking data natively, so you can build AEO monitoring agents, automate reporting, or enrich your existing SEO workflows without manual exports.

Q
Why do I need to optimize specifically for answer engines?
A

According to our research, ChatGPT already generates 0.11% of traffic per month on average, which is nearly 4x higher than its 0.03% average in 2024. This type of traffic will continue to grow. If you aren’t mentioned or linked to in answer engine responses, you miss the opportunity to get referral traffic. You also lose out on reaching users who use these tools to help with their searches. Answer engine optimization keeps your brand part of the conversation and SE Ranking’s AEO Tool is one of the best answer engine optimization tools to support you in this process.

Q
Can I measure ROI from answer engine optimization campaigns?
A

ROI measurement for AEO is still challenging because the space is quite new. But with SE Ranking’s AEO Tool, you can track how often your brand is mentioned, linked, and ranked in AI answers and how those metrics change over time. This lets you connect your AEO efforts to visibility growth and business impact.
