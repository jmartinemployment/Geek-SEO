What is an AI Writer?

An AI Writer is a software that uses artificial intelligence to create high-quality, optimized content. These tools are powered by LLMs, which are trained on vast amounts of text data to understand and mimic human writing styles.

Can AI-written content hurt my website's SEO?

Typically, no. But low-quality or manipulative content generated by AI certainly can. The idea is to combine AI with human insights, fact checking, and editing. In no way should it fully replace human writers. That’s why we strongly recommend using our AI content generator to support and not replace your expert knowledge and writing skills. Make use of its variety of templates, optimization suggestions, and customization options, and you should be good.

Is your Text Generator a free AI tool?

Our AI content generator is not a free tool. It is included in SE Ranking’s Content Marketing add-on. To use the add-on, you need to purchase a basic SE Ranking subscription starting at $65/mo. Pro and Business plans already come with minimal limits for content features. Add-on plans start at $29/mo for 15 articles/mo. The calculation goes as follows: $65 + $29 = $94. This means that for less than $100 a month, you get the AI Writing Generator and SE Ranking’s core SEO tools in one package.

How does SE Ranking’s AI Writing Tool work?

SE Ranking’s AI Text Generator uses a combination of different smart algorithms to make the content creation process as efficient and reliable as possible. More specifically, it’s trained on a vast amount of data to generate engaging (and relevant) topic ideas, build logical structures for content, and write SEO-friendly texts in various formats. The tool is updated regularly to ensure it operates based on the latest technologies available.

How is SE Ranking’s AI Writer different from other content generators?

Unlike many other tools, SE Ranking’s AI Writer combines advanced AI tech, in-depth SERP analysis, and a powerful scoring algorithm—plus seamless integration with key SEO tools. It’s easy to use and delivers well-optimized, compelling content that stands out form the countless articles published per day.

What are the different ways to use the AI Writer?

You can use the AI Writer in two ways: a step-by-step wizard that helps you set keywords, tone, and structure to create an optimized draft, or a one-click mode that instantly generates a full article based on your keyword.

What are the functions of an AI Writer?

An AI Writer can help you save time by quickly generating high-quality text based on your input:

Create articles, headlines, outlines, and FAQs.
Write meta titles and descriptions.
Make your content SEO-friendly by using keyword data.
