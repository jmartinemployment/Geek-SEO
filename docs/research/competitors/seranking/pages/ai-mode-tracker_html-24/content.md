FAQ
Q
Which software can help measure my brand’s visibility in AI Mode?
A

Google itself doesn’t offer direct tools for tracking your visibility in AI Mode. To get this functionality, you’ll need a dedicated solution like SE Ranking. Our tool allows you to track results in AI Mode, assess competitor visibility within this feature, monitor trends over time (like link presence dynamics and traffic forecast), and more.

Q
What’s the best way to benchmark against competitors in AI Mode?
A

The best way to do this is by using third-party tools designed for automated data collection and analysis of AI-generated results. One such tool is SE Ranking’s SEO Tracker for AI Mode that gives you insights into their visibility in AI Mode, top-performing content, and more.

Q
What’s the difference between AI Overviews and AI Mode tracking features?
A

AI Mode appears for all search queries, while AI Overviews are only triggered for select keywords. This means that when an AI Overview shows up, it provides an extra layer of insight. Since AI Mode and AI Overviews rely on different sources and follow distinct patterns, tracking both is essential for a complete view of your AI visibility in Google search.

Q
How accurate are AI Mode rankings compared to classic SERPs?
A

Our AI Mode Tracking Tool captures results directly from live AI Mode conversations, then maps position, citation order, and link placement. We specifically developed this tool to account for the volatility and user-level personalization in AI Mode results. The short answer? You get highly dependable, actionable insights.

Q
Can I track mentions in AI Mode without tracking links?
A

Yes. SE Ranking lets you track mentions in AI Mode, even when your website isn’t directly linked. This helps with understanding brand visibility in conversational responses and optimizing for better citations.
