How can this course help you?

Understand how AI search engines like Google’s AI experiences, ChatGPT, Perplexity, and others generate answers and surface brands.

Learn how to measure your brand’s presence in AI answers using SE Ranking’s AI Search Toolkit, including AI Results Tracker, AI Search in Competitive Research, and the standalone SE Visible.

Discover how to compare your AI visibility with competitors and identify prompts where you’re missing from AI results.

Connect AI search insights with your ongoing SEO strategy, reporting, and client communication, so AI visibility becomes a regular part of your workflow.
