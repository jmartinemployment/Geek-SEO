What is SEO competitor website analysis?

Competitor website analysis is a process in which you examine your website competitors to understand their online strategies, strengths, and weaknesses. To begin, identify the main competitors ranking against you in search. Then, review their organic and paid traffic, keywords they target in SEO and PPC campaigns, backlinks as well as ads. Our competitor website analysis tool collects all this PPC and SEO competition data and much more and displays it in a convenient format.
