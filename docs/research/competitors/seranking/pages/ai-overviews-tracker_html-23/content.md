FAQ
Q
In which countries does the AI Overviews Rank Tracker work?
A

SE Ranking’s AI Overviews Tracker supports all countries where Google’s AI Overviews are available. This includes major markets like the United States, United Kingdom, Germany, Netherlands, Belgium, Spain, Portugal, and many others.

We’re continuously expanding coverage as Google rolls out AI Overviews to new regions. You can find the full list of supported countries in our Knowledge Base article.

Q
How do Google SGE and AI Overviews differ?
A

Both provide answers (generated by Google AI) to users’ search queries and appear at the top of the SERP. SGE (Search Generative Experience) is the term Google initially used to refer to its AI-driven search feature. Google now refers to this feature as AI Overviews.

Q
Will users see AI Overviews for all queries after performing a Google search?
A

No, AI Overviews will not appear for all queries. The range of keywords that trigger AI-generated answers is currently limited. Our recent research shows that AI snippets appear for 64% of keywords. These generally include long-tail search queries where Google AI can provide relevant answers. SE Ranking’s AI Overviews Tracker shows the keywords you rank for that have AI-generated answers. It also displays their search volume.

Q
How can I track AI Overviews?
A

Google doesn’t offer tools to track AI-generated results, but SE Ranking’s new generative AI rank tracking solution does. Use it to track the presence and performance of your keywords in AI Overviews, review sources featured in AI snippets, examine cached copies of SERPs, and analyze the impact of AIOs on your SEO.

Q
What is the quickest way to compare my traditional rankings to AI snippet rankings?
A

Use SE Ranking’s Generative AI Tracker. It provides side-by-side comparisons to show where you stand in both traditional search results and AI Overviews. Choose a keyword to see its positions, dynamics, and other SEO metrics. You can use this information to optimize your SEO game plan.

Q
Can I see traffic from AI Overviews?
A

Google doesn’t have plans to show clicks for AI Overviews in GSC. But SE Ranking does! Our AI Overviews Tracker estimates your potential traffic from AI Overviews. This data helps you predict the potential outcomes of your SEO efforts.
