FAQ
How do I check my SERP position?

You can enter your desired keyword into search and manually check your position for it in the SERP. However, it is more effective to use automatic SERP checkers. Just add your keyword to prompt the tool to collect the top pages ranking for it. You can also specify the URL that you want to use in the analysis. If you notice that your website isn’t appearing on top-ranking pages, you can investigate your competitors and gather ideas for improving your content.

How can I check SERP for free?

You can try SE Ranking’s SERP tool for free. This search engine rankings checker allows you to check the top Google search results for multiple keywords in any location. You can use it to analyze the SERP and check your website position for relevant keywords. Just enter a keyword, choose any region, and define the depth of your analysis.

Why should you use a SERP Checker tool?

Using a SERP checker allows you to:

Analyze competitor performance in the SERPs and uncover SEO strategies driving top rankings.
Evaluate on-page elements such as content length, keyword density, meta tags, and readability.
Check page experience metrics, including page load speed and Core Web Vitals, to enhance user engagement.
Review off-page data, including the number of backlinks and referring domains, Domain Trust score, and more.
Compare your metrics side-by-side with top competitors.
What SEO metrics can you analyze with a SERP Checker?

SE Ranking’s SERP Checker offers an extensive set of metrics, including:

Content & on-page metrics: Keyword density, word count, readability, meta tags, and content structure.
Page experience factors: Core Web Vitals, loading speed, visual stability, and text-to-HTML ratio.
Domain metrics: Domain age, Domain Trust score, and the number of referring domains and backlinks.
SERP-level insights: Content score and search position.
