FAQ
How much does the certification exam cost?

The certification exam is free of charge. This makes it accessible to everyone interested in validating their SEO skills and enhancing their professional credentials without any financial burden.

How do I prepare for the SE Ranking certification exam?

Leverage the preparation materials provided, such as the Help Center, blog, academy, YouTube channel, webinars, and workshops. These resources offer comprehensive guides, tutorials, and expert insights to help you understand the exam content thoroughly.

Do I need an active SE Ranking account to take the exam?

Yes, an active SE Ranking account is required to take the exam. This ensures that you have full access to all SE Ranking tools and features necessary for the certification process.

How long is the certification valid?

The certification is valid for one year. After this period, you will need to retake the exam to ensure your knowledge and skills remain up-to-date with the latest SEO practices and SE Ranking tools.

In what languages is this exam offered?

The exam is available in English.

What does the exam cover and are certain topics more important than others?

The exam covers SEO and Digital Marketing Proficiency (25%), SE Ranking Tools Proficiency (50%), and Client Management and Communication Skills (25%). This distribution ensures a comprehensive assessment of your abilities in critical areas.

How many questions is the exam?

The exam consists of 60 multiple-choice questions. These questions are designed to thoroughly evaluate your understanding and proficiency in the covered topics.

What is the passing grade for the exam?

The passing grade is 80%. This ensures that only those with a solid grasp of the material receive certification, maintaining the high standards of the program.

How long does the exam take?

You have 120 minutes to complete the 60-question exam. This time frame is designed to be sufficient for you to demonstrate your knowledge without undue pressure.

What if I fail the exam?

You can retake the test 7 days after a failed attempt.

How is the exam created?

The SE Ranking Certification Exam was created by SE Ranking team in partnership with Boulder SEO Marketing agency. To ensure accuracy and industry relevance, it was reviewed by leading SEO professionals Carlos Sánchez, Patrick Heijmans, and Connor Wilkins.
