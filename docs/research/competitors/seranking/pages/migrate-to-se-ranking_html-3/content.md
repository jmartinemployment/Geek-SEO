FAQ
Q
Can I migrate from Semrush, Ahrefs, AccuRanker, or AgencyAnalytics?
A

Yes. SE Ranking supports migration from all four. Though what you can bring over and how depends on your specific data set. If you want to map it out before you start, our team can walk you through it.

Q
Can I transfer historical ranking data?
A

Yes, where the source tool supports data export and the format is compatible. From some platforms, you can import historical ranking data directly. For other tools, historical data may be transferred through file-based imports. We’ll tell you upfront what’s recoverable before you commit to anything.

Q
What if my current tool is not listed on this page?
A

Get in touch with our team. We’ll review what your current tool exports, identify the most practical import route, and tell you what transfers and what doesn’t. Most tools produce data in formats SE Ranking can work with, but the specifics matter, and we’d rather map that out with you before you start than after.

Q
Will I need to set up anything manually?
A

Some elements will. Projects and keyword data transfer in most cases, but things like custom report layouts, dashboard configurations, and tool-specific workflows don’t have direct equivalents and will need to be rebuilt in SE Ranking. We flag these upfront so you can plan for them

Q
Can I migrate one project first?
A

Yes, and for most teams managing multiple clients or projects, that’s the approach we’d recommend. Start with one migration, validate that the setup works the way you need it to, then roll out the rest with a clearer picture of what to expect.

Q
Is migration self-serve or assisted?
A

Both options are available. If your data is in a standard format and your project structure is straightforward, SE Ranking’s import tools handle most of it independently. If you’re managing multiple clients, non-standard exports, or want someone to validate the setup before your team goes live, our team works through it with you.

Q
Can I move data from spreadsheets or custom exports?
A

Yes. SE Ranking supports bulk keyword imports from CSV, including tags, groups, and target URLs. If your data is in a non-standard format or spread across multiple files, our team can help you structure it correctly before importing.

Q
What should I do after migration?
A

Start by validating your setup. Review imported keywords, rankings, locations, and project settings. Then adjust your reporting and tracking workflows in SE Ranking. If you’’re moving multiple projects, migrate one first, confirm it works as expected, then roll out the rest.
